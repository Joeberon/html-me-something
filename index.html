My New York Trip

In the fall of 2012, I was given an amazing opportunity to spend a week in New York City with my theatre fraternity called Alpha Psi Omega.

Empire State Building

Our fist big immersion into the city was making it to the top of the Empire State Building where we were able to look across the city. We took in the sights and bright lights that enveloped us.

Time Square

The heart of this beautiful city is Time Square. Hotdogs, street performers and color lights and advertisements encircled us. We still found time to take a group picture.

The Met

Finally we got to explore the Metropolitan Arts Museum and had the priveledge to see beautiful works from the Egyptian to Far East.
